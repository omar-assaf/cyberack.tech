Welcome to the new **CybeRack** technical blog. This space is dedicated to exploring and documenting solutions in
cybersecurity, automation, cloud infrastructure, and advanced scripting.

Our goal is to provide high-quality, technically rigorous content that helps engineers solve real-world problems.
Whether you're configuring complex policies in Microsoft Intune, scripting automation pipelines in PowerShell,
or designing zero-trust network architectures, you'll find relevant insights here.

## What to Expect

In the coming weeks and months, we will be publishing deep-dive articles covering:

1. **Cybersecurity Architecture:** Implementing Zero Trust, endpoint protection, and security posture management.
2. **Cloud Infrastructure:** Azure, Entra ID, and modern device management with Intune.
3. **Automation & Scripting:** PowerShell and Bash scripts for automating tedious infrastructure tasks.

## Open Source and Collaboration

This blog is open source. You can find the code and content on our

We welcome contributions, bug reports, and topic requests via our issue tracker.

Stay tuned for more technical content!
